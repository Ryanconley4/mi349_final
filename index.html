Software Topics of Interest

Big Data

Data Compression

Technology drives our world today. It is integrated into education, work, entertainment, and even our social lives. With that being said, it is inevitable that a massive amount of data is created and utilized every day. It is estimated that new data is produced in the quintillions each day, a figure that is difficult to even wrap your head around. Therefore, it becomes increasingly important to be able to manage and analyze this information effectively. Smart data management and analysis is important as it saves entities critical time and resources.

Data compression goes hand in hand with managing big data. This can involve different types of processes such as storing data in a space effective manner or developing methods to transfer data points between storage effectively. When you consider that big data can be composed of millions and even billions of data points, data compression becomes crucial to working with the data set as even a small increase in efficiency can have a massive effect on the overall runtime.
